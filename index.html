Hello and welcome! My name is Eva Chitul and I will be your virtual host for this website. Within these humble pages you will find a bit of info about yours truly: the ins, the outs and a few fun facts.

To begin with, I will start with a brief background. I was born in Bistrita, a town in northern Transylvania, Romania. And before you ask, yes - Transylvania is awesome! I highly recommend it (post pandemic of course).

I am one of the so called Children of the Revolution - among those born in 1989 before the December Revolution took place and the Communist regime came to an end.

I left Romania when I was 18, right after we joined the European Union, and went to study in the UK (one of the many perks the EU brought us). I followed a BA in International Relations and Politics at the University of Essex, and after graduating I continued with a Masters in EU International Relations and Diplomacy at the College of Europe in Bruges.

After my very EU focused studies, the most logical next step was to move to the heart of all EU things - Brussels. For the past 10 years I have been working in EU affairs, both within consultancies as well as private companies. As part of my work I focused on different areas of EU legislation, including healthcare, transport, digital, electronics, consumers and environment.

I am however very excited to start this new (tech) chapter in my career, and I very much look forward to meeting you and working together! In the meantime feel free to get in touch or to drop me a line!
