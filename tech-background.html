Tech Background

My first touchpoint with programming dates back my highschool years. My major was Programming & Mathematics, and I spend the most part of 4 years getting familiar with C++ and C#. As highschool was coming to an end, the Baccalaureate approaching and as I was facing the all important questions What do I want to study? What do I want to do? I took the decision to continue on a very different path for university: be adventurous, leave Romania and study something completely unheard of - International Relations.

My facination with the tech world did not dissapear over the years and I often found myself wondering whether that all faithful decision to pursue a different path was the right one. As the years passed, the nagging feeling that I need to go back to programming intensified, and with that I began researching and planning a return to my coding origins.

The start: the all mighty Python! In between reading books, attending bootcamps and following online tutorials, I felt I needed a more organised and structured approach to learning, and enrolled into a afterwork/weekends course with the Informal School of IT in Romania. And then, Switchfully.

I am beyong excited to begin learning Java and I look forward to a long career as a developer.
