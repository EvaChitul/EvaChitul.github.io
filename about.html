About

Transylvania

One always wonders if the legends are true. The land of mystery, castles and a lot of bad Hollywood movies - that is my Transylvania. A direct translation of the word would be The Land Beyond the Forest. And we have a lot of those all around the Carpathian mountains that surround the region. The area - due to bad politicians and a lot of corruption - has not developed as it should've. On the plus side, this means its raw feel is still intact. Definitely worth a visit for those seeking an adventure!

Snowboarding

A recently developed passion of mine is that for snowsports, specifically snowboarding. I took up this amazing challenge quite late in my life - at the ripe age of 25 - and have been hooked ever since. I am still in the beginning stages of the journey and getting more confident with each ride.

The pandemic has taken a few snowboarding opportunities from us this past year, but here's to hoping we will find our way back to the slopes soon! And even better, as a group if there are other fellow snowsports enthusiasts among us!

Chess

I have been playing chess for as long as I can remember. The first documented photo/video of this event dates back to about 1991, from my second birthday, where all eyes were focused on me setting the board. Quite the event! My family has a history of chess players - both my grandfather and my father. My father actually played in chess tournaments when I was little.

Sadly, I have not been consistent with chess throughout the years, but every now and then a game is more than welcome. I found Netflix's 'The Queen's Gambit' particularly interesting - and a small reminder of my chess filled childhood. If you have not yet seen it, I highly recommend it!

Slash

Lastly, I would like you to meet Slash, our little mischievous furball. We got her since she was a little kitten (2 months). Her name is a fun word play on both me and my partner's passion for coding and the corresponding /, as well as Guns 'N' Roses guitarist: Slash (you know ... black hair everywhere). She has quite a personality and is polite as can be - she says hello everytime she enters a room.

I have always considered myself to be more of a dog person, but honestly, there's something about cats that gets you hooked. Hopefully you will get a chance to meet her some day!
